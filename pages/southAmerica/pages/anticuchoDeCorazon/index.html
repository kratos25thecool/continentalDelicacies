Anticucho de corazon

Anticuchos are a popular street food in Peru and are generally seen being served from stalls or during outdoor events. They were developed during the times of colonization when the Spaniards adapted the local llama skewers with some of their flavors and changed the meat to beef.

Source: https://www.chimuadventures.com/blog/2018/08/food-south-america/
