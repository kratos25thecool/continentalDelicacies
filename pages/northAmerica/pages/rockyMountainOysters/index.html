Rocky Mountain Oysters

Rocky Mountain Oysters is a formal term for fried bull testicles. Maybe sometimes when the testicles are fried, it does look like a fried oyster, but it isn't a oyster. Not even close. They say that it is good for the heart and muscles because it does have a lot of calories and protein.
