80's Soft Rock

Soft Rock also known as light rock and adult-oriented rock is a derivative form of pop rock that originated in the late 1960s in the U.S. region of Southern California and in the United Kingdom. The style smoothed over the edges of singer-songwriter and pop rock relying on simple, melodic songs with big, lush productions.

Air Supply | Making Love Out Of Nothing At All

Making Love Out Of Nothing At All is a power ballad written and composed by Jim Steinman and first released by Australian soft rock band Air Supply for their 1983 compilation album Greatest Hits. This is the group's last Top Ten hit in the United States.

Rod Stewart | I Don't Want To Talk About It

I Don't Want to Talk About It is a song written by Danny Whitten. First recorded by Crazy Horse, it was Whitten's signature tune. However, it gained more fame via its numerous cover versions, especially the one by Rod Stewart in 1975. In the United States, the cover became a top fifty hit on the Billboard Hot 100 in early 1980.

Chicago | Hard To Say I'm Sorry

Hard to Say I'm Sorry is a song written by bassist Peter Cetera and producer David Foster, for the group Chicago. It was released on May 16, 1982, as the lead single from the album Chicago 16. The song reached No. 1 for two weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 chart on September 11 of that year. It was the group's second No. 1 single.

Electro Music

Electro or electro-funk is a genre of electronic music and early hip hop directly influenced by the use of the Roland TR-808 drum machines and funk. Records in the genre typically feature drum machines and heavy electronic sounds, usually without vocals. It is less vocal-oriented and more focused on electronic beats produced by drum machines.

Feed Me | Lonely Mountain

Jon Gooch aka Feed Me debuted in 2008 as the first external artist to be featured on Deadmau5’s revered mau5trap label. Unique brand of hard-hitting, bass-heavy electronica. This song is from the album Calamari Tuesday released on 14 October 2013 as the first release on Gooch's own label Sotto Voce.

Daft Punk | Around the World

"Around the World" is a song by the French electronic music duo Daft Punk. The song released as the second single from their debut studio album, Homework (1997). It became a major club hit around the world and reached number one on the dance charts. The song's only lyrics are "around the world" said 144 times.

Porter Robinson & Madeon | Shelter

"Shelter" is a song by American DJ and record producer Porter Robinson and French DJ and record producer Madeon. The song and music video was released and uploaded to Porter's YouTube channel on August 11, 2016 garnering 60 million views. The song was released in the United States on Columbia Records and popculture as a digital download.
